My focus is within the UX design field but being a new professional I am happy to go into design or development roles. I can currently fill UX, UI design and front end development roles.

SKILLS AND TECHNOLOGY

UX DESIGN

I am a firm advocate of the agile sprint methodology when it comes to the UX design of a product. I like to collaborate with clients initially to gather a base knowledge to expand into accurate user personas. Empathizing with the end user allows me to create goal oriented systems avoiding as many pain points as possible. I like to prototype early and often and constantly reiterate a product to create an objectively successful solution.

What I am confident doing without guidance:

  • SWOT analysis & market research
  • User research collection (surveys, interviews, focus groups, product usage observation)
  • User research analysis (quantitative & qualitative)
  • Client workshopping (needs - goals diagrams, personas, empathy mapping)
  • Creating personas
  • Information Architecture/Content heirarchy
  • Functionality list
  • User journey map
  • UX wireframing
  • Prototype usability testing
  • Prototype creation
  • End user task analysis
