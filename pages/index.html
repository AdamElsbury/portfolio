SKILLS AND TECHNOLOGY

UX DESIGN

I am a firm advocate of the agile sprint methodology when it comes to the UX design of a product. I like to collaborate with clients initially to gather a base knowledge to expand into accurate user personas. Empathizing with the end user allows me to create goal oriented systems avoiding as many pain points as possible. I like to prototype early and often and constantly reiterate a product to create an objectively successful solution.
